Property tax assessment notices mailed this week

What the notice means, how to read it, and how to file an appeal if you believe your assessment is incorrect.

DO Department of Finance

City of Birmingham · June 18, 2026

Annual assessment notices are mailed to property owners citywide.

Property owners across Birmingham will receive their annual assessment notices in the mail this week. The notice states the assessed value of your property and is used to calculate property taxes; it is not a bill.

How to read your notice

  • Assessed value — the value assigned to your property for tax purposes
  • Exemptions — any homestead or other exemptions already applied
  • Appeal window — the deadline to dispute the assessment
  • Contact — where to get help understanding the notice

If you believe your assessment is inaccurate, you have the right to file an appeal before the stated deadline. The Department of Finance can walk you through the process and the documentation needed.

The notice is not a bill — it’s your assessed value, and you have a window to appeal it.
